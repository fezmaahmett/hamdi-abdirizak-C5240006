Help & Instructions

How to Use the Music Playlist Organizer

  1. Search for Songs: Use the search bar on the Home page to find songs from our music library.
  2. Add to Playlist: Click the "+" button on any search result to add a song to your playlist.
  3. Play Music: Click on any song in your playlist to start playing it.
  4. Player Controls: Use the play/pause, previous, and next buttons to control playback.
  5. Edit Songs: Click the edit icon (‚úé) on any playlist item to modify its details.

  7. Share Playlist: Click the "Share Playlist" button to generate a shareable link.

Form Validation Rules

  • All fields marked with * are required
  • Song duration must be in MM:SS format (e.g., 3:45)
  • Audio URL must be a valid web address starting with http:// or https://
  • Song title must be at least 2 characters long
  • Artist name must be at least 2 characters long

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues:

  • Clear your browser cache and refresh the page
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection for searching songs
  • Check that your browser supports the HTML5 audio element
  • For sharing issues, ensure you're not in private/incognito mode
